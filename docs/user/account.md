# Account Management

![Image](./account.png)

## Creating an Account

To start using EpiTrello, you need to create an account:

1. Visit the EpiTrello homepage
2. Click the **Sign Up** button in the navigation bar (or use the "Get Started" button)
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name**: Your display name
   - **Email**: Your email address (must be unique)
   - **Password**: Minimum 6 characters
   - **Confirm Password**: Must match your password
4. Click **Create Account**

If registration is successful, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

### Registration Validation

- All fields are required
- Email must be valid and not already registered
- Password must be at least 6 characters long
- Passwords must match

## Signing In

If you already have an account:

1. Click **Sign In** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

You'll be redirected to your dashboard upon successful login.

### Session Management

- Your login session lasts for 7 days
- After 7 days, you'll need to sign in again
- Sessions are stored securely in httpOnly cookies

## Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personal dashboard showing:
- Your name and email
- A logout button

*Note: Additional features like boards and tasks are planned for future releases.*

## Logging Out

To log out of your account:

1. From the dashboard, click the **Logout** button
2. You'll be redirected to the homepage
3. Your session will be cleared

## Troubleshooting

### "Email already exists"
This email is already registered. Try signing in or use a different email address.

### "Email or password incorrect"
Check that you're using the correct email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive.

### "Server connection error"
The server may be temporarily unavailable. Check your internet connection and try again.
